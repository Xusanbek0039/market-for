Return Policy

Return for Users

We appreciate your support and strive to provide the best products and service possible. This Return Policy is applicable to all purchases made through our website.

1. Right of Withdrawal (Cooling-off Period)

As a consumer in the European Union, you have the right to withdraw from the contract within 14 days without giving any reason.

The withdrawal period will expire after 14 days from the day on which you acquire, or a third party other than the carrier and indicated by you acquires, physical possession of the goods.

2. Returns

We accept returns within the following conditions:

  • For standard products (keyboards), returns are accepted within 14 days of the purchase date.
  • For custom-made products (handmade keycaps), returns are not accepted unless the product is faulty or does not conform to the agreed specifications.
  • The item must be in its original condition and packaging.
  • Proof of purchase is required.

3. Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

If your refund is approved, it will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your credit card or original method of payment, within 14 days.

4. Shipping

Shipping costs are refundable only in the case of withdrawal within the 14-day cooling-off period. If you receive a refund due to withdrawal within this period, we will also reimburse the standard delivery costs from us to you.

If you are returning items outside the 14-day cooling-off period or for reasons other than withdrawal (e.g., faulty or non-conforming goods), the cost of return shipping will be your responsibility, and it will be deducted from your refund.

If you have any questions about our Return Policy, please contact us:
