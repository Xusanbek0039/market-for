Warranty Policy

Warranty policy for Easy Keyboard Maker

Last Updated: 2023-12-12

1. Warranty Coverage:

  • Easy Keyboard Maker offers a limited warranty on keyboards and keycaps sold directly through our official website or authorized resellers.
  • The warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship under normal use for a period of 6 months from the date of purchase.

2. What Is Covered:

  • Defects in materials and workmanship.
  • Keycaps' fading or discoloration under normal use.

3. What Is Not Covered:

  • Damage resulting from accidents, misuse, abuse, or unauthorized modifications.
  • Normal wear and tear, including but not limited to scratches or cosmetic damage.
  • Products purchased from unauthorized sellers or outlets.

4. Making a Warranty Claim:

  • To make a warranty claim, please fill out our contact form here.
  • Include the following information in your claim:
    • Proof of purchase (order number and date).
    • Clear photos or videos showing the defect.
  • Easy Keyboard Maker will review the claim and, at its discretion, provide a replacement or issue a refund.

5. Exclusions and Limitations:

  • The warranty is non-transferable and applies only to the original purchaser.
  • Easy Keyboard Maker reserves the right to refuse warranty coverage if the claim does not meet the specified conditions.

6. Shipping Costs:

  • Customers are responsible for any shipping costs associated with returning the product for warranty evaluation.

7. Modification of Terms:

  • Easy Keyboard Maker reserves the right to modify or terminate the warranty policy at any time. Customers will be notified of any changes.

8. Contact Us:

  • If you have any questions about our warranty policy, please contact us here.

By purchasing products from Easy Keyboard Maker, you acknowledge that you have read and agreed to the terms and conditions of this Warranty Policy.
