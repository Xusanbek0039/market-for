Shipping policy

EU & World Wide Shipments

Europe Shipments

Standard Shipping: All orders are shipped via standard shipping and typically take 5-7 business days to arrive.

Expedited Shipping: Expedited shipping options are available at an additional cost, providing faster delivery within 2-3 business days.

Order Processing: Orders placed before 2pm GMT will be shipped out the same day. Orders placed after 2pm GMT will be shipped out the next business day.

Customs and Import Duties: Please be aware that we are not responsible for any customs or import duties that may be charged on orders shipped outside of the UK. The recipient is responsible for paying any additional charges for customs clearance.

Rest of the World Shipments

Standard Shipping: All international orders outside of Europe are shipped via standard shipping, and delivery times may vary depending on the destination. Please allow extra time for customs clearance.

Expedited Shipping: Expedited shipping options are available for international orders at an additional cost, providing faster delivery. Delivery times may vary based on the destination country.

Order Processing: Orders are processed promptly, and shipping times will depend on the chosen shipping method and the destination.

Customs and Import Duties: International customers are responsible for any customs or import duties that may be charged by the destination country. The recipient is responsible for paying any additional charges for customs clearance.

Thank you for choosing us for your keyboard needs! If you have any questions about our shipping policy, feel free to contact us.
