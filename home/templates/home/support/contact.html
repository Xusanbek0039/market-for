Frequently Asked Questions

Explore FAQs about our retail keyboards and custom keycaps. Find answers to common queries on orders, customization, and product maintenance.

Why is my order taking so long?

We apologize for any delay in your order. Our team is working diligently to process and dispatch orders promptly.

DHL's standard delivery times apply, and we appreciate your patience as your products make their way to you. If you have specific concerns, feel free to contact our support team for further assistance.

How do i get in touch?

You can reach us through various channels:

  • Fill out our Contact Us form on the website.
  • Join our Discord server for real-time assistance and community interactions.
  • Email us at "support@easykeyboardmaker.com" for any specific requests or inquiries.
  • Connect with us on social media for updates and news: follow us on Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram.

We're here to help and also share exciting updates with our community!

What is your return policy?

We offer a 15 to 30-day return policy. For more details, including how to initiate a return, please visit our Return Policy page.

Is my personal information secure?

We take the security of your personal information seriously. Our website employs industry-standard encryption and security measures to safeguard your data. In compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we prioritize the privacy and protection of your information. For more details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.
